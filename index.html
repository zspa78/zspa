ABOUT ME

I love soap

Soap designs are many, and the ability to manipulate the texture employs a wide variety of possible techniques. For example, thin soap can be swirled in the mold to create a spin swirl. On the other hand, extremely thick soap can be piped and manipulated to create shapes and texture. If you can dream it, you can probably soap it! I hope you enjoy this design directory, and find it helpful and inspiring! It has helped me identify which techniques I need to showcase more.

Angelina Paiva
